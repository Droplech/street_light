LED street lamp ROSA ISKRA LED 30W

ROSA LED luminaires are characterized by a unique design, an innovative LED light source and anodizing technology.
In their production, special attention is paid to workmanship, durability and aesthetics.

ROSA LED luminaire housings are made of high-quality aluminum alloy profiles and are anodized.
Anodizing protects the aluminum body from corrosion and aggressive external influences such as acid rain, sea water, UV
radiation and at the same time gives the aluminum a perfect aesthetic appearance. The anodized layer besides aesthetic advantages,
increases heat transfer of the case that allows to support low temperature of light-emitting diodes and to increase their durability essentially.

The ROSA ISKRA LED luminaire is available in one standard size with two powers: 30 and 39.5 W, and is intended for cantilever
mounting (mounting on a console (bracket / headrest) with a 60 mm end).

For seamless installation of the ISKRA luminaire, a special support with a 5 m high bracket ROSA SAL DL-10 or head
restraints type WR-10 and WR-10 / P is available.

When using the ROSA KA-10 bracket this lamp can be used as a street sconce
